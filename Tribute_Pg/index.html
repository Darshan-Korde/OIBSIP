About Ratan Tata

Ratan Naval Tata is an Indian industrialist, philanthropist, and former chairman of Tata Sons. He led the Tata Group from 1990 to 2012 and again as interim chairman from 2016 to 2017. Under his leadership, Tata Group reached new heights with acquisitions like Jaguar Land Rover and Tetley Tea.
